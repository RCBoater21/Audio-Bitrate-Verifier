FFPROBE / FFMPEG
================

Audio Bitrate Verifier can download and invoke ffprobe from the FFmpeg project.
FFmpeg is not bundled in this package. When requested by the user, the app
retrieves a Windows build from:

https://github.com/BtbN/FFmpeg-Builds
https://ffmpeg.org/

The downloaded runtime is governed by its own licenses. Applicable license and
notice files from the upstream archive are extracted into the runtime folder.
